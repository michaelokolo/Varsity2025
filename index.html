VARSITY 2025

Welcome To The
Humber Games 2025!

This year we’re celebrating the 10th anniversary of Varsity!

With over 80 fixtures, over 25 student groups and around 1,600 students, Varsity is one of the biggest competitions of the year. This is a chance for students from the University of Hull and the University of Lincoln to compete for the coveted Varsity trophy in a full day of competitions.

On Saturday 29th March Lincoln will be hosting Varsity 2025.

This webpage will be your guide to everything you will need to know about the day, including locations, timings and the Varsity After Party.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • LINKS – St John Ambulance Unit – A group of Hull University Students’ Union volunteers that form LINKS as part of St John Ambulance unit. They can provide medical help. They will be on duty during the After Party in Asylum.
  • HUSU President Team – Keep an eye out for Hull University Students’ Union President Team. Say hello if you see them and they would be happy to help you.
  • Security – There will be a number of security staff on campus, both during the day and at the After Party in Asylum

  • If you lose any of your belonging during the day, please refer back to where you lost the item in the first instance. For example, the Allam Sport Centre, Wetherspoon at Sanc, SPAR or Asylum. Any items left in the bagdrop will be loaded on the bus back to Lincoln.
  • Consumption of alcohol or participants being under the influence while taking part in sporting activity is forbidden. If this is the case, the opposition will receive the win and the team/individual involved could face disciplinary action.
  • Consumption of alcohol by spectators is forbidden in unlicensed areas. If you are seen drinking alcohol, you will be asked to get rid of it. If you do not dispose of it and/or you are openly intoxicated, you will be asked to leave the premises. You may also be subject to HUSU’s disciplinary processes.
  • Alcohol is strictly prohibited at the Hull Sport Park . There will be security going into Allam Sports Centre and through the revolving gate to the outdoor area where bag searches will be conducted. If you have alcohol on you, it will be conviscated.
  • By agreeing to take part in Varsity, you automatically agree to these rulesis. Alcohol can be purchased and enjoyed in Wetherspoon at Sanc, our on-campus pub located in the Student Central building, after your game.
  • In the event of an accident or incident, notify a member of staff in a hi-vis jacket or a member of staff of the venue. In the event of a building evacuation, report to the appropriate muster point. Please keep your university informed of any incidents.
  • If an emergency occurs at the After Party, please notify a member of staff or a member of security.
  • Lincoln students – you can store your belongings for free on the first floor of Student Central from 8am-3am. Give yourself plenty of time to collect your belongings and retain your ticket for exchange. Any belongings left in the cloakroom at the end of the night will be loaded onto the buses back to Lincoln.
  • Hull Students – we recommend taking any large items or equipment back home with you, but the Asylum cloakroom will be open as usual for any small bags and coats.
  • If you have purchased a Varsity pack, entry to the after party is included in the ticket price. You will need to wear your wristband to gain entry and take your student card and ID with you. If you are an alumni, your name will be given at the door but you will need to show wristband and ID as well. Entry to the after party is £5 advance/£6 on the door.
  • Lost or broken wristbands will not be replaced. If you lose your wristband, you must buy a ticket to the After Party on the door of Asylum. The cost cost £5 advance/£6 on the door.
  • Access to some of our sporting venues require crossing roads. Remember to cross safely, looking both ways.
  • Don’t miss your bus if you are a Lincoln student.
  • If you have any safety concerns reach out to a member of staff.
  • First aid cover will be provided at Hull sport reception. Please fill out the incident form, incident form,here.
  • Don’t drive on campus, please try to use the buses provided or walk onto campus. We are due to have an Offer Holder day on the same day as Varsity so campus will be busy and parking will be limited and reserved for applicants.
  • Lincoln students will be dropped off at Courtyard, on campus. The bus will go one way through the West Entrance and exit through the North Entrance to aid traffic congestion and student safety.
  • Lincoln Students will be dropped off and picked up from the same location.
  • Buses to external venues for spectators and participants will pick up outside Venn/ Middleton Hall bus stops.
