VARSITY 2025

Welcome To The
Humber Games 2025!

This year we’re celebrating the 10th anniversary of Varsity!

With over 80 fixtures, over 25 student groups and around 1,600 students, Varsity is one of the biggest competitions of the year. This is a chance for students from the University of Hull and the University of Lincoln to compete for the coveted Varsity trophy in a full day of competitions.

On Saturday 29th March Lincoln will be hosting Varsity 2025.

This webpage will be your guide to everything you will need to know about the day, including locations, timings and the Varsity After Party.

Food and Water

Wetherspoon at Sanc

Nothing works up hunger like a big match. Our brand-new Wetherspoon at Sanctuary menu offers comfy seating, big TV screens and pub-grub meals and drinks at affordable, student prices. Choose from sharing platters, burgers or street food. There are also vegan options and a gluten-free menu. Visit Wetherspoon website

SPAR

At Spar, you can begin your day with something hot from the bakery and a coffee from the Seattle's Best Coffee machine. Keep energised throughout with a huge choice of snacks and energy drinks. There are also miscellaneous items - electronics, toiletries and University of Hull merchandise. Visit Spar Website

Water

Fill up your water bottle for free! There are water fountains located on the ground floor of Student Central and in the Allam Sports Centre.
